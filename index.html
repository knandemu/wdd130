Kitale-Kenya

Kitale is the county headquater of Trans-Nzoia County, county number 26 in Kenya. Its a nice place with best weather contions suitable for agriculture, this makes it suitable for faming activities hence also refered to as the bread-basket of our nation. The different varieties of farm produce are sold out to feed the whole nation and enven the surplus exported outside Kenya to earn foreign exchange for the nation. Besides farming Kitale is a tourist attraction centre with several wildlife conservation centers like saiwa swamp in Cheranganyi where we have the famous Statungas and several others scartered across the county. Kitale also has produced many famous atheletes who have broken many world records in diffent desciplenes and the most recent one is our son Wanyonyi a luhyia that hails from Saboti which is a sub-county in Trans-Nzoia County. We are proud of Kitale and Trans-Nzoia.
